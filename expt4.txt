<h1><font color=" black"><u>About Us</u></font></h1>
<h3><p>Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - ClearTrip is a pioneer in India’s online travel industry. Founded in the year 2000 by Deep Kalra, ClearTrip came to life to empower the Indian traveller with instant bookings and comprehensive choices. The company initiated its journey serving the US-India travel market offering a range of best-value products and services powered by technology and round-the-clock customer support.
After consolidating its position in the market as a brand recognised for its reliability and transparency, ClearTrip Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - ClearTrip is a pioneer in India’s online travel industry.<br> Founded in the year 2000 by Deep Kalra, ClearTrip came to life to empower the Indian traveller with instant bookings and comprehensive choices. The company initiated its journey serving the US-India travel market offering a range of best-value products and services powered by technology and round-the-clock customer support.
After consolidating its position in the market as a brand recognised for its reliability and transparency, ClearTrip launched its India operations in 2005. With more and more Indians initiating to transact online with IRCTC and new opportunities with the advent of low cost carriers, ClearTrip offered travellers the convenience of booking travel online with a few clicks.
</p><p>ClearTrip’s rise has been led by the vision and the spirit of each one of its employees, for whom no idea was too big and no problem too difficult. With untiring determination, ClearTrip has proactively diversified its product offering, adding a variety of online and offline products and services. ClearTrip has stayed ahead of the curve by continually evolving its technology to meet the ever-changing demands of the rapidly developing global travel market, steadily establishing itself as India’s leading online travel company.</p></h3>
